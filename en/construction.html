Painting, Repair, Renovation & Demolition — All in One.

KANEX provides integrated construction and renovation services for factories, warehouses, offices, and commercial facilities. From painting to waterproofing, scaffolding, demolition, and restoration — we handle the full scope.

FEATURES

KANEX Construction Capability

We go beyond painting — coordinating multiple processes through a single contractor relationship.

Painting + Waterproofing

Combine exterior painting with waterproofing treatment in a single scaffolding mobilization, reducing cost and downtime.

Painting + Surface Repair

Crack and spall repairs handled together with the painting schedule for improved durability and finish quality.

Demolition + Renovation

Interior and exterior demolition followed by renovation and finishing — all managed by one contractor.

Scaffolding + Painting + Restoration

From scaffold erection to painting, ancillary work, and teardown — complete process management by KANEX.

Exterior + Steel + Floor / Pipe

Multiple building components addressed in a single mobilization — efficient and comprehensive for industrial sites.

Reducing General Contractor Overhead

SERVICES

Core Service Areas

Serving factories, warehouses, logistics centers, offices, and commercial facilities.

Exterior & Roof Painting

Repainting of concrete, ALC, and steel structures. Suitable for large industrial and commercial buildings.

Steel, Floor & Ancillary Painting

Fencing, stairs, handrails, grating, and floor coatings including anti-slip and chemical-resistant finishes.

Waterproofing & Sealant Work

Rooftop and balcony waterproofing, exterior sealant replacement and over-banding to prevent water ingress.

Demolition & Partial Renovation

Interior strip-out, exterior removal, and equipment dismantling followed by restoration finishing.

Scaffolding & Elevated Work

Temporary scaffolding erection and dismantling for safe, efficient exterior and rooftop access.

Office & Store Renovation

Fit-out and renovation for offices and commercial spaces, including specification changes and finishing.
