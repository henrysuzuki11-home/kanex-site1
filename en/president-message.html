Message from the President

Thank you very much for visiting the website of KANEX Corporation.

As a company rooted in the local community, we have been committed to meeting a wide range of customer needs through our businesses, including painting, repair, and demolition work for corporate clients, home renovation and exterior-related services for individual customers, and the supply of electronic boards, assembly, harnesses, precision tools, and polishing tools for the manufacturing industry.

What we value most is not simply providing products and services, but understanding what our customers truly need and responding with sincerity, reliability, and steady execution. Drawing on the experience and practical capabilities we have cultivated over the years, we place the highest importance on addressing each inquiry carefully and professionally.

Even as the business environment continues to change, we will remain committed to earning the trust of our customers, business partners, and local communities by valuing quality, responsiveness, and strong human relationships, while continuing to provide better services.

We sincerely appreciate your continued support of KANEX Corporation.

President and Representative Director
Yasuhide Makihara
