CAREERS

Careers at KANEX

We are looking for future management candidates to help shape the next generation of KANEX. We welcome motivated individuals who want to grow alongside the business.

MESSAGE

What We're Looking For

KANEX Corporation is recruiting future management candidates to support our business growth and build the next generation of leadership across our two core business areas.

We're not looking for specialists only. Our ideal candidate wants to develop across field operations, client management, project coordination, procurement, and eventually business management.

We invest in people with long-term potential — not just immediate skill sets. Experience is not required; attitude, motivation, and commitment to long-term growth matter most.

PROFILE

Who We're Looking For

We assess character, motivation, and growth potential — not just prior experience.

ATTITUDE

Mindset & Orientation

—Wants to build a long-term career in a locally-rooted company
—Aims to grow into a management or leadership role
—Interested in participating in business and company development
—Values relationship-building with clients and partners
INTEREST

Areas of Interest

—Wants to work across operations, sales, and management
—Interested in construction, renovation, or manufacturing
—Eager to develop skills across multiple business functions
—Wants to connect field teams, clients, and business operations
ROLES

Expected Responsibilities

You will start with hands-on work to understand the business, then gradually expand your scope.

—Assisting with construction site coordination
—Client meetings with general contractors and property managers
—Scheduling and coordination with subcontractors
—Responding to residential renovation inquiries
—Sales and procurement support for electronics business
—Progression into operations management and business leadership
APPEAL

Why Work at KANEX

01

Broad Practical Experience

Exposure to field operations, sales, procurement, client management, and business coordination — all within a single company.

02

Long-Term Development Path

KANEX is committed to developing its next generation of leadership. We grow with you — not just for immediate output.

03

Two Industries in One Company

Experience both the construction sector and electronics manufacturing support. Cross-industry practical skills build lasting career value.

Let's build the future of KANEX together.

We evaluate character, motivation, and long-term growth potential above all else. If you're interested in joining KANEX, we'd love to hear from you.
